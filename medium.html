For a French luxury house managing sales, stock and clienteling data primarily via Excel, I conducted a flash audit of processes and data flows across supply, retail, e-commerce and clienteling — 10 interviews with operational teams. Direct collaboration with the CEO and Marketing Director. I deployed automation quick wins (scripts, data pipelines), migrated files to the cloud, and built a strategic roadmap toward a centralized data platform with Snowflake.
